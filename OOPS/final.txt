Final keyword
how to restrict the class from being inherited?
sol: declare the class as final.

if we declare the method as final, then we can inherit the class, but we cannot override the final method()
final methods can be inherited but cannot be overridden., 

the variables declared as final, must be assigned a value, when created and that value cannot be changed.
 we cannot declare the final for the abstract class, similarly , we cannot declare the abstract class as final

 final and abstract  , cannot be used at the same time
